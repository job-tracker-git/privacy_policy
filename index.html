Job Application Tracker - Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Job Application Tracker, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that you have full control over your data. Our extension is designed to help you keep track of your job applications without compromising your personal information.

How Our Extension Works

The Job Application Tracker extension is a tool that allows you to manually record and manage your job applications. Here's what you need to know:

  • We do not collect, store, or process any of your personal data.
  • All information about your job applications is stored locally on your device.
  • You have full control over the data you input and can delete it at any time.
  • We do not have access to your browsing history or any other personal information.

Data Storage and Security

Your job application data is stored securely on your own Google account using Google Sheets. We use Google's authentication system to ensure that only you have access to your data. We do not have any ability to view, modify, or delete your information.

Your Control Over Your Data

You are in complete control of your data:

  • You can view, edit, or delete your job application entries at any time.
  • If you choose to uninstall the extension, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.
  • Your Google Sheet will remain in your Google Drive, and you can choose to keep or delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
